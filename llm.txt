# KPI Agent

> An AI-powered research tool for Product Managers and Go-to-Market teams. 
Input any industry and receive a structured list of KPIs that industry uses 
to measure success, the operational data required to calculate each KPI, the 
software systems that typically produce that data, and a confidence signal 
on each result.

Built to solve a real problem: GTM and PM teams entering new industries 
spend hours on manual research before customer conversations. KPI Agent 
turns that research into a single query.
